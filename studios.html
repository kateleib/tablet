STUDIOS

Red Yoga Studio

Red Yoga Studio is located at 421 E Liberty Street. This studio uses therapeutic, safe infrared heat to help you get the most out of your practice. This innovative technology relieves pain, improves flexibility, and detoxifies the body.

Tiny Buddha

Tiny Buddha has a location at 338 South State Street, as well as 1717 Pauline Boulevard. These studios are heated vinyasa studios in Ann Arbor, Michigan. We focus on creating a supportive yet challenging environment for students to breathe, stretch and sweat in.

aUM Yoga

aUM Yoga is located at 1220 South University on the University of Michigan's Central Campus! The name is a play on the Sanskrit word 'aum' (more commonly spelled 'om') as well as Ann Arbor and the University of Michigan. aUM Yoga is an energetic, supportive, and diverse space that brings the holistic practice of yoga to the Ann Arbor community.
